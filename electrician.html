Electrician / handyman work

 Pape is available for small electrician tasks. In Senegal, he is a formally trained, experienced electrician. He has certificates in industrial and building electricity and has experience in new construction and working with existing wiring. As he works to get professional certification in the United States, he enjoys any opportunity to do smaller handyman electric projects.

 His experience working in a developing country has made him very comfortable working with older wiring and "creative" solutions made by previous electricians.

 Here's some of what he can do:

  • Changing outlets and switches
  • Diagnosing shorts and other problems
  • Rewiring to move ceiling lamps
  • Adding an outlet to an unwired porch
  • Removing unwanted lamps, outlets, or switches
  • Installing security cameras and motion detector lights
  • Mounting TV sets
  • And more!
