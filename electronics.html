Custom Electronics Design & Development

 In Pape's family village in central Senegal, there is no electricity, so people have to travel miles on foot or by horse cart just to charge their cell phones. This motivated Pape to branch out from electricity and learn electronics design by watching YouTube videos and reading.

 He has designed and built many portable solar charging stations, with different capacities and capabilities. He also made a portable soldering station. He was motivated to make these to help his family and others living without electricity, but it turns out they're great for camping too.

 If you're interested in having some custom electroncis built for camping or other purposes, get in touch with Pape to discuss project ideas. He'd love to work on something for you.
